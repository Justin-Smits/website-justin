Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

How long does it take to build my website?
+
Typically 1–3 weeks from signed agreement to launch. It depends on the complexity of the project and how quickly you can provide content and feedback. I'll give you a specific estimate in our first call.
Is there a setup fee or upfront cost?
+
No setup fee. Your first monthly payment covers the design and build of your website. Everything is included in the flat monthly rate — that's the whole point.
What if I want to cancel?
+
Starter and Growth plans require a minimum of 6 months, after which you can cancel with 1 month's notice. Pro plans are 6 months minimum. You keep all your content and can export everything.
What counts as a "content change"?
+
Text updates, image swaps, adding a product, changing prices, updating contact info — anything that takes under 30 minutes. Larger structural changes (new pages, new features) are discussed separately.
Do you build webshops and e-commerce sites?
+
Yes — e-commerce is available on the Pro plan, using Shopify or WooCommerce depending on your needs. I'll recommend the right platform in our discovery call.
I'm not technical at all — is that a problem?
+
That's exactly why you're hiring me. I handle all the technical complexity and explain everything in plain language. You focus on your business — I handle the website.
