Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

How long does a website take?
+
Typically 6–12 weeks from signed proposal to launch. Timeline depends on complexity, feedback speed, and content readiness. I'll give you a specific estimate in our first call.
Do I need a Microsoft license for AI automation?
+
For most Copilot Studio and Power Automate solutions, yes. I'll guide you through exactly what you need and help you get the right plan at the right price.
What's included in the €899 Basic Website?
+
A fully custom, responsive website with up to 5 pages, SEO foundations, contact form, Google Analytics integration, and 2 rounds of revisions. Hosting and domain are not included but I'll help you set them up.
Can you build a webshop or e-commerce site?
+
Yes — for e-commerce I work with Shopify or WooCommerce. Pricing depends on the scope. Let's talk in a free call.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
+
Absolutely. Maintenance packages from €75/month including updates, security monitoring, performance optimisation, and content changes. Or book ad-hoc hours as needed.
I'm not technical — is that a problem?
+
Not at all — that's exactly why you're hiring me. I handle all technical complexity and explain everything in plain language. My job is to make the tech invisible.
